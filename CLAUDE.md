# Product

**Motto / core selling point: "Spend yield from your VISA card."**

kasu-mobile is a neobank wallet whose hook is that the **yield earned from lending to
Kasu is automatically topped up onto the user's VISA debit card** — so users spend their
yield directly. Every screen should reinforce this: the Home page is the center of the app
and shows the VISA card, the balance, the portfolio (Total invested + positions), and a
**countdown to the next weekly epoch** with **how much $ yield will be credited to the
card** at that epoch. The lending portfolio is the crucial content — surface it on Home,
not buried in a tab.
